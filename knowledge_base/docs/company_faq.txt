COMPANY FAQ AND POLICIES
========================

RETURN POLICY:
- Customers can return products within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition and packaging
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Electronics can only be returned if defective
- To initiate a return, provide your order number

SHIPPING POLICY:
- Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days
- Express shipping takes 1-2 business days
- Free shipping on orders above $50
- International shipping takes 7-14 business days
- Tracking number provided via email after shipment

ORDER CANCELLATION:
- Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement
- After 24 hours, cancellation is not guaranteed
- Cancelled orders are refunded within 3-5 business days
- Contact support with your order number to cancel

PAYMENT METHODS:
- We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- PayPal and Google Pay accepted
- Cash on Delivery available in select areas
- EMI options available for orders above $100

WARRANTY POLICY:
- Electronics come with 1 year manufacturer warranty
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only
- Physical damage is not covered under warranty
- Warranty claims require proof of purchase
- Extended warranty available for purchase

CUSTOMER SUPPORT HOURS:
- Monday to Friday: 9 AM to 6 PM
- Saturday: 10 AM to 4 PM
- Sunday: Closed
- Email support available 24/7
- Response time: within 24 hours

DAMAGED ITEMS:
- Report damaged items within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of damaged item and packaging
- Replacement or full refund will be provided
- No return shipping required for damaged items

ACCOUNT ISSUES:
- Password reset available via email
- Account locked after 5 failed login attempts
- Contact support to unlock account
- Personal data can be deleted upon request
